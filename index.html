About

EV Delhi is a startup founded in 2002 by a student of Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University For Women. Since it’s inception, the company has left no stone unturned to contribute towards a greener and more sustainable Delhi by making EV charging stations easily accesible and conveniently reachable for the public. EV is the future of automobile industry and EV Delhi is here to make the EV maintainance a smooth process. We have a team of highly experienced individuals in their respective fields working tirelessly to fulfill our vision of clean and pollution-free Delhi.In the upcoming years, we will be spreading our stations across the country for a sustainable and clean India.
